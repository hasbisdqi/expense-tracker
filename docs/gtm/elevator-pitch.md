# Expense Tracker — Simple, private money tracking

Expense Tracker helps you capture expenses in seconds and see where your money goes — and it never sends your data anywhere. Everything stays on your device: no accounts, no cloud, no trackers.

- Capture fast: quick entry, smart suggestions, tags, categories, and receipt photos so logging is effortless.
- See the story: clear charts, weekly/monthly summaries, and your top spending categories at a glance.
- Truly private: works fully offline and stores all data locally — we never collect or transmit your information.
- Take control: export your data any time (CSV/JSON) and personalize categories, currencies, and themes.

Perfect for people who want powerful, thoughtful money tools without giving up privacy. No signup, no ads, no surprises — just your money, yours alone.
